About US

India International School is a globally recognised pioneer in academic achievements with its enlightened approach and commitment to excellence. It was the second enterprise of India Educational Society. It was established in 1990 and is a collaborative effort with the India International Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. It is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi and offers quality education from Nursery to Class XII. It also lays equal emphasis on the proficiency of a learner in the acquisition of essential life skills attitudes, values, and achievement in outdoor co-curricular activities encompassing sports and games.
